About Us

Who Fivnex is, and how we started

Founded June 17, 2020 and incorporated [NOT INCORPORATED YET], Fivnex aims to support open-source projects, and build a better life. Our founder, Kai Lyons, originally formed Fivnex to host their projects to where collaboration is easier. Fivnex is so much more than that, now. Our aim, for now and forever, is to push the limits of software and invent astonishing things. We support technologies that will be a powerhouse of the future, and we will work to build. We will also work with and fund other open-source projects, whether financially or with hardware.
