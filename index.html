Our Mission

A short message on the mission of Fivnex

Fivnex is a development group founded on commercializing open-source software. Everything we make is open-source to some extent. That is our promise. Fivnex will aim to be a for-profit, but we also want to give back as much as possible. We want to focus our earnings through donations under a future Fivnex Foundation, coming when we incorporate in 2021. We do not want to be a for-profit company, and what money we don't spend on projects will be donated to other non-profits in the open-source and privacy communities, as well as open-source projects.

Fivnex wants to also aim to further women, people of color, and LGBTQIA+ people in technology. We strive to best include LGBTQIA+, different races and nationalities, and multiple genders and gender identities. This is not an empty promise, like other companies and groups. Our CEO is Pansexual/Polyamourus and Non-binary, and multiple staff members are LGBTQIA+ and non-whites. We also want to fight back discrimination in the workforce and diversify STEM and technology. Note, we still hire people based on ability above all else, while as bringing more LGBTQIA+, women, and people of color is a thing we strive to do, we also don't want to push ANY form of discrimination.
